## How to Use

### Starting a Conversation
1. Click the floating microphone button (bottom-right corner)
2. OR Tab until you hear "Start AI conversation" and press Enter
3. Wait for "Connected" message
4. Start speaking naturally

### Having a Conversation
Just speak naturally! Examples:

**Product Discovery:**
- "I need a good pair of headphones for working out"
- "Show me laptops good for video editing under $2000"
- "What's the best-rated gaming mouse you have?"

**Shopping Actions:**
- "Add this to my cart and take me to checkout"
- "I want 3 of these, add them to cart"
- "Remove that speaker from my cart"

**Getting Help:**
- "Tell me more about the features"
- "Is this worth the price?"
- "Compare this with the Sony headphones"
- "What do reviewers say about battery life?"

**Complete Workflows:**
- "Search for laptop, show me the highest rated one, add it to cart, and take me to checkout"
- "Show me my wishlist, add the first item to my cart, and proceed to checkout"

### Ending a Conversation
- Say "goodbye" or "bye" or "stop listening"
- OR Click the red microphone button
- OR Tab to button and press Enter

## Best Practices

### For Blind Users
- **Be specific**: "Search for Bluetooth speakers" works better than "I need audio stuff"
- **Ask follow-ups**: "Tell me more", "What about battery life?", "How much?"
- **Multi-step is fine**: "Add this to cart and take me to checkout"
- **Natural language**: Speak like you're talking to a person

### For Power Users
- Chain commands: "Search X, filter by Y, sort by Z, show me the top result"
- Be conversational: The AI understands context from previous messages
- Ask for explanations: "Why do you recommend this?", "What's the difference?"

## Troubleshooting

### "AI not responding"
- Check if microphone icon shows in browser address bar
- Grant microphone permission if prompted
- Check browser console for errors (F12)
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting

### "AI doesn't understand"
- Speak more clearly or rephrase
- Be more specific with product names
- Try simpler commands first

### "Actions not working"
- Check if you're logged in (cart/wishlist require authentication)
- Verify you're on the right page (e.g., need product page to add to cart)
- Check browser console for error messages

## Privacy & Security
- All voice processing happens through OpenAI's secure API
- No voice recordings are stored permanently
- Your conversations are not saved
- Authentication is required for cart/wishlist/orders
- Payment info is never processed via voice
